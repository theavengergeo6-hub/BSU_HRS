────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
A1. SYSTEM OVERVIEW
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

The BSU Hospitality & Venue Reservation System (HVRS) is an online platform
that lets individuals and organizations reserve two types of facilities at
BatStateU ARASOF-Nasugbu:

  1. FUNCTION ROOMS  — Venues such as Function Room A, B, C, D, and E,
                       designed for seminars, meetings, training sessions,
                       and similar events.

  2. GUEST ROOMS     — Accommodation rooms and dormitory spaces for overnight
                       or multi-night stays.

KEY FEATURES FOR CLIENTS:
  • No account/login required — reservations are open to everyone.
  • Real-time availability calendar before you book.
  • Guided 7-step reservation form that collects all needed information.
  • Automatic email confirmation sent to your inbox upon submission.
  • Email updates each time the admin changes your reservation status.
  • Transparent pricing shown before you submit.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
A2. BROWSING THE WEBSITE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Open your web browser and navigate to the BSU Hostel website. The homepage
features:

  • A rotating banner (carousel) highlighting available facilities.
  • An "About Us" section with the hostel's mission and vision.
  • A FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions) accordion for common inquiries.
  • A navigation bar at the top with links to all main pages.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
A3. CHECKING ROOM & VENUE AVAILABILITY (CALENDAR)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Before making a reservation, always check the calendar to confirm your
preferred dates are free.

HOW TO USE THE CALENDAR:
  1. Click "Calendar" in the navigation menu.
  2. The calendar displays the current month by default.
  3. Use the LEFT ( < ) and RIGHT ( > ) arrows to navigate between months.
     You can also use the month/year dropdown selectors for quick navigation.
  4. Each date cell shows colored dots or badges:
       • GREEN dot  = Confirmed/Approved booking on that day
       • YELLOW dot = Pending booking (waiting for approval)
       • PURPLE dot = Pencil-booked (tentatively held) date
  5. Click on any date cell to see detailed event information for that day,
     including the event name, time, venue, and requester.
  6. Use the filter checkboxes to show/hide Approved, Pending, or
     Pencil-booked events.

TIP: If a date you want is completely free (no dots), it is available.
     If a date shows events, still proceed — different rooms may be free.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
A5. WHAT HAPPENS AFTER SUBMISSION
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

After you submit your reservation, the following process takes place:

  STEP 1 — SUBMISSION
    Your reservation is saved in the system with status: PENDING.
    A confirmation email is automatically sent to your email address.

  STEP 2 — ADMIN REVIEW
    The hostel administrator reviews your request. This is done through
    their Admin Panel.

  STEP 3 — STATUS UPDATE
    The admin will change your status to one of the following:

    ┌─────────────────┬──────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
    │ Status          │ What It Means                                        │
    ├─────────────────┼──────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
    │ PENDING         │ Your request has been received and is being reviewed  │
    ├─────────────────┼──────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
    │ PENCIL BOOKED   │ Your date has been tentatively held. This is a       │
    │                 │ soft reservation — not yet fully confirmed.          │
    │                 │ Further communication with admin may be needed.      │
    ├─────────────────┼──────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
    │ APPROVED /      │ Your reservation is CONFIRMED. Prepare for your     │
    │ CONFIRMED       │ event/stay as scheduled.                            │
    ├─────────────────┼──────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
    │ DENIED /        │ Your request was not approved. The admin may        │
    │ CANCELLED       │ have included a reason in the notification email.   │
    └─────────────────┴──────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

  STEP 4 — EMAIL NOTIFICATION
    Every time your status changes, you will receive an automatic email
    informing you of the update.

A7. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (CLIENT)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q: Do I need to create an account to reserve?
A: NO. The system is open to all. You only need to fill in your contact
   details in the reservation form.

Q: Can I reserve multiple function rooms for one event?
A: YES. In Step 2, you may select multiple rooms. Each room will have its
   own schedule entry in Step 3.

Q: What is "Pencil Booking"?
A: A pencil booking is a tentative hold on your preferred date. It means
   the admin has noted your request but has not yet fully confirmed it.
   You may need to reach out to admin to progress to full approval.

Q: How do I know if my reservation is confirmed?
A: You will receive an email notification whenever your status changes.
   Look for the "APPROVED" or "CONFIRMED" status email.

Q: What is the check-in and check-out time for guest rooms?
A: Standard check-in is at 2:00 PM. Standard check-out is at 12:00 PM
   (noon). Contact the admin for special arrangements.

Q: Can I cancel my reservation?
A: Contact the hostel admin directly to request a cancellation. Refer to
   the Terms & Conditions you accepted during booking for the cancellation
   policy.

Q: Who do I contact for questions?
A: Email: hostel.nasugbu@g.batstate-u.edu.ph

B4. RESERVATIONS CALENDAR (FUNCTION & GUEST ROOMS)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  TAB 1: FUNCTION ROOMS
    Displays all function room bookings for the selected month.

  TAB 2: GUEST ROOMS
    Displays all guest room reservations for the selected month.

Switch between tabs by clicking the buttons at the top of the page.

HOW TO NAVIGATE THE CALENDAR:
  1. Use the LEFT/RIGHT arrow buttons to move between months.
  2. Use the month and year dropdown selectors for quick jumps.
  3. Color-coded dots on each date cell indicate:
       • GREEN (Approved/Confirmed)
       • YELLOW (Pending)
       • PURPLE (Pencil Booked)
  4. Click on any date cell to open a POP-UP MODAL showing all events
     for that specific day.

EVENT MODAL (POP-UP):
  Each event card in the modal shows:
    • Event/activity name
    • Status badge (Approved / Pending / Pencil Booked)
    • Time (start — end)
    • Venue(s) / Room(s) — multiple rooms shown as a comma-separated list
    • Requester name
    • Number of participants
    • Office or organization
    • A "View Details →" link to see the full record

FILTER CHECKBOXES:
  Use the filter bar above the calendar to show/hide events by status:
    ☑ Approved   ☑ Pending   ☑ Pencil Booked
  Uncheck any category to temporarily hide those events.

SEARCH BAR:
  Type an event name or requester name in the search box to quickly find
  a specific reservation. Results appear as a dropdown list of matches.
  Click a result to go directly to that reservation's detail page.

STATS CARDS (above the calendar):
  Four summary cards show totals for the current month:

"Pencil Booking" is used when you want to tentatively hold a date for a
client, without giving full confirmation yet. Common use cases:

  • The client needs to finalize payment or documentation first.
  • You want to block the date while coordinating with other departments.
  • There is a scheduling conflict that needs resolution.

  1. Click the PURPLE "Pencil" (✏) button on the reservation card.
  2. A confirmation modal appears.
  3. Click "Yes, Confirm" to pencil-book the reservation.
  4. The system sets the status to "pencil_booked."
  5. The card moves from "Pending" to the "Pencil Booked" tab.
  6. An email is automatically sent to the requester notifying them.

NOTE: Pencil-booked reservations still appear on the calendar with a
      PURPLE dot, so staff can see the tentative hold.

B14. KEY REMINDERS & BEST PRACTICES
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  DOUBLE-BOOKING PREVENTION
    ✦ ALWAYS open the Reservations Calendar before approving a request.
    ✦ Check the specific date and room against existing approved/pencil
      bookings before confirming a new one.
    ✦ One function room can only be used by one event at any given time.

  COMMUNICATION
    ✦ Use the "Admin Remarks" field to communicate directly with requesters
      when changing status — especially when denying a request, always
      include a brief explanation.
    ✦ Clients receive your remarks in their email notification.

  PENCIL BOOKING USAGE
    ✦ Use pencil booking when a date needs to be held but cannot yet be
      fully confirmed (e.g., awaiting payment or document submission).
    ✦ Follow up with clients in a timely manner to either confirm or release
      pencil-booked dates.

  EMAIL SYSTEM
    ✦ Ensure the email configuration is set up and tested (Settings page).
    ✦ All status update emails are sent automatically — staff do NOT need
      to manually email clients when updating a reservation status.
    ✦ If email fails to send, the status still updates in the system.
      Check debug.log for email errors if needed.

  PDF FORMS
    ✦ Print or download PDF forms for approved reservations and keep
      physical copies as required by your office's filing procedures.

  REPORT EXPORTS
    ✦ Export monthly CSVs from the Reports page for official use,
      budget reporting, or submission to management.

  MOBILE ACCESS
    ✦ The admin panel is responsive and accessible via smartphones/tablets.
    ✦ You can approve or deny reservations on-the-go using a mobile browser.
    ✦ Tap the ☰ menu icon to open the navigation sidebar on mobile.

  LOGOUT
    ✦ Always log out when you are done, especially on shared computers.
